# Level 3 Pages Section Summary

These pages are more supporting or conversion-oriented pages that help visitors confirm trust, browse content, or resolve questions before contacting the agency.

## 1. `faq.html`

### Main sections
- Banner / page title

- FAQ accordion
  - What it tries to tell: "The visitor can understand the service model, delivery process, and business expectations without needing direct contact first."
- CTA banner
  - What it tries to tell: "If the FAQ is not enough, the visitor should reach out and talk to the team."
- Testimonials
  - What it tries to tell: "The brand is trusted by clients, which reassures new visitors."

## Overall Level 3 page intent

Level 3 pages are mostly support and confirmation pages. They help the website:
- answer objections,
- prove trust,
- help users discover content,
- recover from broken navigation,
- and review incoming leads or partnerships.

These pages support the broader conversion process by reducing uncertainty and keeping the user moving through the website.
