## Overall Level 2 page intent

Level 2 pages are more specialized pages intended to:
- explain one service in detail,
- tell the visitor what the offer includes,
- show why the agency is the right choice,
- support conversion through packages, testimonials, and FAQs,
- and guide the visitor deeper into the site.

These pages act as the main “sales detail” layer of the website.
